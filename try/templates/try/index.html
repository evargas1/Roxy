What is the difference

Dietitian

A registered dietitian is a nutrition professional who is the most credible and objective source of nutrition information. They are trained in nutrition to prevent disease. Registered Dietitian Nutritionists (RDN) must complete a four year program in Nutrition from an accredited University.

Nutritionist

In clinical practice, a specialist in nutrition. Nutritionists can help patients with special needs, allergies, health problems, or a desire for increased energy or weight change devise healthy diets. Some nutritionists in private practice are well- trained, hold a degree and are licensed.
